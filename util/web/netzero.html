Netzero

The Netzero is an adjustment-free put butterfly with the following guidelines. There is also a course.

Guidelines

Underlying: Not specified, we take ^RUT

Entry: Delta-neutral
  • 70 dte
  • 1 (upper) long put closest to delta 60
  • 2 short puts closest to delta 40
  • 1 (lower) long put closest to delta 20
Exit

We tested the following values:

  • Delta change of 37.5, 50 and 100 percent (originally 30%)
  • Exit at 14 dte (originally ≤ 30)
  • TP at 10 and 50 percent
