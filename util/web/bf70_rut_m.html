BF70

The BF70 is an adjustment-free broken wing butterfly that is similar to the Road Trip Trade. The guidelines are explained here (in German).

Guidelines

Underlying: ^RUT

Entry
A new trade is entered every month, on the date closest to the specified DTE.
  • 70 (65 - 75) DTE
  • 1 (upper) long put at the money
  • 2 short puts 30 points below the upper long strike
  • 1 (lower) long put 40 points below short strike
Exit
  • TP: $1000 > 35 DTE; $800 > 28 DTE; $600 > 21 DTE; $400
  • ^RUT is 10 points below short strike
  • DTE < 40 and ^RUT is 60 points above short strike
  • DTE < 7
The criteria above always apply. The chart shows all permutations of the following additional entry and exit criteria:
  • C - "Cheap": Entry for $1.10 or less
  • D - "Downday": Entry on a down day (< 0%)
  • P - "Patient": Entry above 75 - 65 DTE
  • E - "Expected value": exit if expected value < current pnl
  • X - No criterion applied
