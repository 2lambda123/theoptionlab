The Bull

"The Bull" is a 30 points wide bull put credit spread for a minimum of $2.50. The guidelines are explained here.

Guidelines

Underlying: ^RUT

Entry:
A new trade is entered every month, on the date closest to the specified DTE.
  • 65 DTE
  • 1 short put closest to delta 10
  • 1 long put 30 points below
  • Minimum credit of $2.50
Exit
  • 37 or 30 DTE; when the back expiration is at 65 DTE
  • Stop Loss: when credit doubled
    • We tested several take profit and stop loss settings:
    • P - "Profit": Take Profit at 50% or 80% of credit, and X (none)
    • L - "Loss": Stop Loss at 2x or 4x credit, and X (none)
    The original version is P_L_200, without take profit and when the credit doubled.
