The Bull

"The Bull" is a public trading strategy of John Locke. The guidelines are explained here.

Guidelines

Underlying: ^RUT

Entry: 30 points wide bull put credit spread for a minimum of $2.5
  • 65 dte
  • 1 short put closest to delta 10
  • 1 long put 30 points below
Exit
  1. 37 or 30 dte; when the back expiration is at 65 dte
  2. SL: when credit doubled
We first tested several take profit (TP) and stop loss (SL) criteria, with the following values:
  • TP: 50% or 80% of credit, and X (disabled)
  • SL: 2x and 4x credit, and X (disabled)
The original version is P_X_L_200, without take profit and when the credit doubled.
